Nowadays, we use AI in many things. For example, two weeks ago, I asked AI for information about a scientist, and it provided me with a lot of information, including her family, age, picture, places where she studied, and a complete biography. I googled her and apparently, she isn't real!

Our project aims to solve the problems associated with creating data using AI. We want to help you identify what information is real and what isn't.

How do you do this?

In our project We've created a database of articles where their truthfulness is unknown; they're not labeled as true or false. Some of these articles are true, and some are false. You can view them in the gallery page. We use a function called 'check' to calculate the likelihood of an article being true. This function includes checking the date, verifying information through Google, and using AI tools.
